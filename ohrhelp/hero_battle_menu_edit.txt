This menu allows you to customize the battle menu for a hero.

A preview of the battle menu is shown in the top right corner of the screen.

Use the up and down arrow keys to select the battle menu item you want to customize, and press ENTER or SPACE

You can add new battle menu items with INSERT and you can remove battle menu items with DELETE

Hold SHIFT and press the up and down arrow keys to re-order the menu items.

Note the preview of the menu to the right ignores tag conditions, assuming all items are enabled.

The default battle menu is:

  Weapon
  Spell List 0
  Spell List 1
  Spell List 2
  Spell List 3
  Items
