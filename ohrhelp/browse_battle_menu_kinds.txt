Pick which kind of menu item will be in the battle menu.

Weapon:
  Use the attack selected by the item
  the hero currently has equipped as
  a weapon.

Specific Attack:
  Use a specific attack, by ID number

Spell List:
  Use one of the hero's spell lists.
  The list must be named if you want
  it to appear in the menu.

Items Menu:
  Let the hero use items from the
  items menu.
